AQI Information

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a measure of how polluted the air is, based on levels of several different types of pollutants, including ozone, particulate matter, nitrogen dioxide, and sulfur dioxide. The AQI is typically reported on a scale of 0 to 500, with an AQI of 0 indicating perfect air quality and an AQI of 500 indicating extremely hazardous air
