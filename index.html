Why is soil quality analysis important?

Understanding soil type is crucial for agriculture. Different crops thrive in specific soil compositions.This project helps farmers optimize their land for better yields and contributes to sustainable agricultural practices.

What does the Air Quality Index (AQI) tell us?

he AQI is a numerical value that indicates how polluted the air is and how it might impact human health. A low AQI signifies clean air, while a high AQI suggests potential health risks.

Does this project provide real-time air quality data?

The air quality analysis relies on existing weather and air quality APIs, which update data at regular intervals. This provides a good snapshot of current air quality but may not reflect real-time fluctuations.

How can air quality impact soil health?

Air pollution can negatively impact soil health. Acid rain caused by air pollutants can alter soil pH levels, making it less suitable for plant growth. Additionally, some pollutants can settle on the soil surface, hindering essential oxygen exchange and potentially harming soil microbes.
